Don't worry! Here comes Pokemon Go!

Pokemon Go is an awesome game designed for people of all ages made by the high-tech company Google assisted by Nintendo.It begins with a simple animated and less detailed version of the highly popular Google Maps. But that's not all to it. Pokemon, little creatures made to look like tiny monsters start appearing on the screen. But they don't come every minute. And they won't come if you stay in one spot. You have to move around. Common Pokemon can just be found by driving along your local road, but Pokemon with high CP(Conbat Power) are found in grassy locations or somewhere-only-Google-knows. You level up, buy items in stores, and the best part is catching the Pokemon you see with PokeBalls and then battling them in Gyms with your color-coded team.
